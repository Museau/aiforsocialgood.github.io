Amine Bellahsen is a research master student at Polytechnique Montréal in the applied mathematics and computer science departments. His current research consists on developing a deep learning based assistant in medical imaging to support radiologists on their therapy procedures. Before that, Amine attended a preparatory school in Morocco, then pursued a master in applied mathematics at Télécom SudParis engineering school and he is currently pursuing a double degree at Polytechnique Montréal.
