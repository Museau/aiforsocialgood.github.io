Eric is Head of Quantitative Strategies and Asset Allocation at the World Bank Treasury, in charge of the design of quantitative financial solutions for capital markets, investments and sovereign policies. He provides advisory to central banks, sovereign or pension funds and Ministries of Finance. Co-organizer of the BIS/World Bank Public Investors Conference, the Public Debt Management Conference, and member of the working group for the G20 Investor Forum (Nov. 2018, Buenos Aires). He holds a PhD in Finance from Cass Business School and a Magistere in International Economics and Finance from University of Bordeaux.
